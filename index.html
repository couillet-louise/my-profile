Gozo Island

Man if you want to go to Gozo Island, I would recommend that you rent a car! It took us 1h of bus to go to the ferry, then 30min of Ferry and after again 1h of bus to reach the first beach we wanted to visit. But you can see by the pictures that again, it was totally worth it.
